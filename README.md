In today’s digital era, people need quick and easy access to healthcare information. Many individuals ignore symptoms or delay consulting a doctor due to busy schedules, lack of awareness, or limited medical access. To overcome this problem, Healmate AI Health Symptom Checker is developed as an intelligent healthcare solution.

 This system uses Artificial Intelligence to analyze user symptoms and provide possible health condition suggestions. It also includes an AI chatbot that interacts with users, answers health-related queries, gives precautionary advice, and guides them on the next steps.

 HealMate aims to provide fast, user-friendly, and accessible healthcare support anytime and anywhere, helping users make better health decisions
